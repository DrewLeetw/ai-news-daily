# About AI News Daily

This site is a **daily curated digest** of the most important AI news, papers, and product launches from the previous day.

## How it works

Every day at 10:00 AM (Asia/Taipei), an autonomous agent:

1. Scans top AI sources (arXiv, HuggingFace, OpenAI, Anthropic, Google AI, Meta AI, GitHub Trending, Hacker News, etc.)
2. Filters for the most significant events
3. Writes a bilingual summary (English + 繁體中文)
4. Publishes to this site automatically

## Sources

- arXiv (cs.AI, cs.CL, cs.LG)
- Official AI lab blogs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google DeepMind, Meta AI, Mistral, xAI)
- Hacker News (AI-tagged stories with >100 points)
- GitHub Trending (AI/ML repositories)
- Major tech press (TechCrunch, The Verge, Reuters)

# 關於每日 AI 新聞

1. 掃描主要 AI 來源（arXiv、HuggingFace、OpenAI、Anthropic、Google AI、Meta AI、GitHub Trending、Hacker News 等）
2. 篩選最重要的事件
3. 撰寫雙語摘要（英文 + 繁體中文）
