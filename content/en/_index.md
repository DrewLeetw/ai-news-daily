## How this works

Each post covers the **previous day's** significant AI events:

- 🧠 Major model releases (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, Mistral, xAI, etc.)
- 📄 Notable research papers on arXiv
- 🚀 New product launches
- 💼 Funding & acquisitions
- 🛠️ Open-source milestones

All summaries are written in **bilingual format** (English + 繁體中文) for easy review.
