# About AI News Daily

A daily curated digest of the most important AI news, papers, and product launches from the previous day.

## How it works

Every morning at 10:00 AM (Asia/Taipei), an automated agent:

1. Scans major AI sources (arXiv, HuggingFace, OpenAI, Anthropic, Google AI, Meta AI, GitHub Trending, Hacker News, etc.)
2. Filters the most important events
3. Writes a bilingual summary (繁體中文 + English)
4. Publishes to this site

## Content structure

- **Home**: latest digest on the front page
- **Archive**: all historical digests for easy review
- **About**: this page

## Sources

- arXiv (cs.AI, cs.CL, cs.LG)
- Major AI lab blogs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google DeepMind, Meta AI, Mistral, xAI)
- Hacker News (AI-tagged stories >100 points)
- GitHub Trending (AI/ML projects)
- Major tech press (TechCrunch, The Verge, Reuters)
- Third-party AI news aggregators (buildfastwithai.com, etc.)
